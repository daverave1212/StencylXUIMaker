	
	
	Maneuvers
	
[Dash] : 1 Action
You can move 4 more meters this turn.

[Defend] : 1 Action
Until the start of your next turn, your Armor and all saves increase by +2.	

[Push] : 1 Action
A creature within 1 meter makes a Fortitude or Dexterity save (its choice).
If it fails, it is pushed 1 meter away from you.
You can always Push even if you don't have this maneuver, but the target has Advantage on the save.

[Shift] : 0 Actions
You can move 1 meter without triggering attacks of oportunity.
This turn you can't move normally (and you can't use this if you already did).

[Bull Rush] : Passive
If you move at least 3 meters in a straight line and then attack a target, the attack gains +1 to hit.

[Taunt] : 0.5 Actions
Make an enemy roll a Wisdom save.
If it fails, its next attack will (most probably) be on you.
You can always Taunt even if you don't have this maneuver, but the target has Advantage on the save.
1 meter range.

[Footwork] : Passive
You can't be slowed.

[Grapple] : 1 Action
Attempt to grapple an enemy.
You make a Fortitude roll and the enemy makes a Dexterity or Fortitude roll (its choice).
If you roll equal to or more than the enemy, the enemy becomes Grappled.
A Grappled enemy can't move on its turn, has 0.5 fewer Actions and can't attack you.
You and the enemy repeat the roll at the end of each of that enemy's turn.
On your turn, you must spend 1 Action to keep the enemy Grappled. If you don't, the enemy is released.
You can always Grapple even if you don't have this maneuver, but the target has Advantage on the roll.

[Hamstring] : 1 Action
A creature within 1 meter makes a Dexterity save.
If it fails, its maximum speed becomes 3 meters.

[Disengage] : 1 Action
You don't trigger attacks of oportunity this turn.

[Aim] : 0.5 Actions
This turn, your next normal attack which takes 0.5 Actions has Advantage on the hit roll.

[Wait] : 1 Action
Increase or decrease your initiative by 5 this encounter.

[Overthrow] : 1 Action
Spend 1 Charge.
A creature near you makes a Fortitude save and you make a Fortitude check.
If you roll more than that creature's save, deal 3d4 + 2 damage to it and throw the creature over your shoulder, on the opposite side of your position, next to you.
	
	Basic Spells
	
[Acid Burst] : 0.5 Actions
A creature within 3 meters makes a Dexterity save.
It takes 1d8 + 1 Acid damage on a failed save.

{Poison Spray} : 0.5 Actions
A creature within 3 meters makes a Dexterity save.
It takes 1d8 + 1 Poison damage on a failed save.
(Acid Burst variant.)

[Burning Breath] : 0.5 Actions
All creatures in a 90 degree, 3 meter cone take 3 Fire damage.

[Bonfire] : 1 Action
Choose a 1x1x1 meter zone within 6 meters.
A bonfire errupts in that place that lasts up to 10 minutes.
Any creature touching the bonfire takes 1d10 Fire damage.
If the bonfire can spread fire to nearby flammable objects that aren't being worn or carried, it spreads at the start of your next turn (same for further bonfires).



[Cleansing Flames] : 0 Actions
Spend 1 Charge.
Make a spell attack against a target up to 9 meters away.
If it hits, deals 2d4 Fire damage and removes all buffs and debuffs from the target.

[Conjure Ammo] : 1 Action
You can conjure up to 10 arrows, bolts or bullets, javelins, throwing knives, etc.
The ammo disappears after 10 minutes.
Tip: If you have this, you can just say you keep X amount of conjured ammo always ready, to speed the game up and not constantly interrupt the game.

[Control Flames] : 1 Action
Choose a 1x1x1 meter cube of fire.
You can do the following things to it:
- You can spread the fire 1 meter in any direction
- You can extinguish it
- You can double of halve the radius of light or heat it spreads
- You can change its color
- You can cause simple shapes to appear in the flicker.
You can have 2 effects active at the same time at level 1, 3 at level 4 and 4 at level 8.
Effects last up to 1 hour.
If the fire touches a creature, it takes 1d8 Fire damage.
6 meters range.

[Control Water] : 1 Action
Choose a 1x1x1 meter cube of water or ice.
You can do the following things to it:
- You can move it 1 meter in any direction
- You can roughly alter its shape
- You can change its color or opacity
- You can freeze or evaporate the water. This will take up to 5 minutes at level 1 for 1x1x1 meter cube of water.
At level 8, you can evaporate or freeze 1x1x1 meter cube of water with 1 Action.
The water does no damage if it touches a creature.
Effects last up to 1 hour.
6 meters range.

[Control Earth] : 1 Action
Choose a 1x1x1 meter cube of dirt, loose stone, sand, etc.
You can do the following things to it:
- You can move it 1 meter in any direction.
If a creature was hit by it or it was removed from below a creature's feet, it rolls a Dexterity save (against your Spell DC).
If it fails, it takes 1d8 damage and is knocked prone.
If a creature is burried in dirt (or loose stone, etc) after failing the save, it takes 1d8 additional damage.
- You can roughly shape it in any form
- You can cause it to become difficult terrain.
You can do this twice per Long Rest at level 1, 3 times per Long Rest at level 4 and unlimited times at level 8.
6 meters range.

[Dancing Blade] : 1 Action
You magically sheathe out a 1-Handed weapon you are carrying.
For 1 minute, you can attack with that weapon (it levitates and attacks).
This has 2 meters range.

[Detect Poison and Disease] : 1 Action
You know if there is any poison (or poisonous creature) or disease (or ill person) within 6 meters.

[Eldritch Blast] : 1 Action
Make a Spell attack.
If you hit, deal 2d6 + 3 Force damage.
6 meters range.
	
[Fire Bolt] : 1 Action
Make a Spell attack.
If you hit, deal 3d8 - 2 Fire damage.
3 meters range.

{Inflict Wounds} : 1 Action
Make a Spell attack.
If you hit, deal 3d8 - 2 Necrotic damage.
3 meters range.
(Fire Bolt variant.)

[Frostbite] : 1 Action
A creature within 6 meters makes a Fortitude save.
Roll 2d8.
If it fails, it takes the rolled damage (Cold) and its movement speed is halved for 1 turn.
Otherwise, it takes half of that damage.

[Guidance] : Reaction
When an ally within 6 meters makes an ability check, it can use your modifier instead of its own.
You can only do this every 1 hour.

[Hollow Touch] : 1 Action
Make a Spell attack.
If you hit, deal 1d12 + 1 damage (Necrotic) and the target is unable to reagain health until the end of its next turn.

[Land Breach] : 1 Actions
You gather stone, dirt, sand, etc from the surroundings and release it upon an enemy.
A creature within 9 meters makes a Dexterity save.
If it fails, it takes 2d8 + 3 Force damage.

[Flameblade] : 0 Actions
Change the damage type of a melee attack you landed to Fire damage.

[Light] : 1 Action
You create a magical light bulb that sheds light in a 6 meters radius (20 meters at Level 5).
The bulb levitates and stays where you put it, and you can always move it around with your hands.
You can also simply attach the light to any object.
Lasts for 1 hour (8 at level 9), or until you cancel it or cast Light again.
You choose the color of the light.
_The bulb can't go through solid objects._

[Magic Missiles] : 1 Action
Shoot 3 missiles. Each missile does 1d4.
At Level 4, you can also spend any number of charges, and you shoot 2 more missiles for each Charge spent.
You can send missiles to different targets, but you must declare all the attacks at once.

[Mastery] : 1 Action
For 1 minute, you can add your Wisdom or Charisma to an attack roll with a weapon instead of Strength or Dexterity.

[Mind Blast] : 1 Action
Make a Spell Attack against a creature and it also rolls a Wisdom save.
If you hit, deal 2d10 - 1 Psychic damage.
If it fails, it must move 1 meter in any direction (its choice).

[Purify Food and Drink] : 1 Action
Food you touch becomes clean of microbes and poison.
A water containter you touch (up to 2 liters per cast) becomes drinkable, clean and free of microbes and poison.

[Prestidigitation] : 1 Action
6 meters range.
You can create harmless sensory effects.
You can create sparks and change the temperature of objects (channeled).
You can instantly clean or soil objects.
You can make symbols appear on surfaces (up to 1 hour).
You can create small, harmless illusions that fit into your hand.
You can control a quill with telekinesis to quickly write with it (1 meter range).

[Resistance] : 1 Action
Touch an ally.
It gains +1d4 on a saving throw of your choice.
Lasts until their next rest.
You can only have this buff active on one person at a time.

[Quicksand] : 0.5 Actions
Declare a 2x2 meter zone within 6 meters made of dirt, sand or other such material.
That zone becomes Difficult Terrain. Any creature on it is Slowed while it's on the zone.
Lasts 1 minute.
_Variant: You can also cause this difficult terrain with vegetation._

[Thaumaturgy] : 1 Action
You create one of the following magical effects (6 meters ranger):
- Your voice booms up to three times as loud as normal for 1 minute.
- You cause flames to flicker, brighten, dim, or change color for 1 minute.
- You cause harmless tremors in the ground for 1 minute.
- You cause an unlocked door or window to fly open or slam shut.
- You alter the appearance of your eyes for 1 minute.
At level 5 you can cast this for 0.5 Actions, and at Level 9, for 0 Actions.


[Thunder Clap] : 1 Action
All creatures within 1 meter of you make a Fortitude save.
If they fail, they all take 1d6 + 1 Thunder damage.

[Encode Thoughts] : 1 Action (Level 4)
You pull a String of Memory or Thoughts (can be visual, auditive, sensory and olphactive) from your own memory.
This String can be up to 1 minute in length.
It is visible to the eye as an arcane ribbon-like energy.
You can transport this String like you would carry a normal 10x10x10 decimeter object.
To view the String, a person can willingly absorb it through the mouth, eyes, nose, etc (corresponding to the type of memories) and visualize the String.
After that, the String disappears.
Encoding another String makes the previous String disappear.
Lasts up to 8 hours. 

[Chaining Grasp] : 0 Actions
Spend 1 Charge.
Extend a magical chain to a solid object or medium (or larger) creature up to 6 meters away and pull yourself to the target instantly.
The target also takes 1d6 True damage.



	Level 1 Spells
	
[Absorb Element] : Reaction
Spend 1 Charge.
Use when you take magic damage.
You only take half of that damage, and you infuse yourself with that element.
The next time you make an attack, that attack deals 1d6 damage of the infused element (if it hits).

[Alarm] : 10 minutes
Spend 1 Charge.
You setup an invisible 4x4x4 meter cube.
If an unknown creature or enemy enters the cube, it alerts you mentally with a bell sound, or whatever sound you choose.
Lasts for up to 8 hours.

[Antimagic Rune] : 1 Action (Level 4)
Spend 1 Charge.
Place a rune on the ground.
You can activate it at any time and from any distance.
When you activate it, the rune creates a 25x25 feet zone.
ALL units inside are attempted a silence and take (Your Level) Arcane damage on detonation.

[Animate Dead] : 1 Action
Spend 1 Charge.
Raise an undead creature to fight for you from a nearby medium or larger corpse. The corpse is then consumed.
The undead creature always has Medium size, 10 Maximum Health and 10 Armor.
It has saves equal to yours, but -2 to all skill checks.
It has 4 meters movement speed.
It can use 1 Action to attack (Main Stat bonus to hit) and deals exactly 5 damage if it hits.
Your undead takes its turn immediately before or after your turn (you choose).
The undead will always try to move to and attack the closest enemy to it (you choose if it's a tie).
You can use 0.5 Actions on your turn to command (one of) your undead to move to a certain location.
Lasts up to 10 minutes.

[Charming Word] : 1 Action
Spend 1 Charge.
A creature within 6 meters makes a Wisdom save.
If it fails, all your Charisma checks for/against it have +2.
Lasts until the creature can't see or hear you anymore.

[Cure Wounds] : 1 Action
Spend 1 Charge.
Touch an ally and heal it for 1d12.

[Cure Poison] : 1 Action
Spend 1 Charge.
Touch an ally and end any ongoing poison effects on it.

[Cure Disease] : 10 minutes
Spend 1 Charge.
Touch an ally and cure a normally curable disease from it.
If the disease is harder to cure (up to DM's interpretation), it costs 2 Charges instead of 1.
Can't cure terminal diseases.

[Dispel Magic] : 1 Action
Spend 1 Charge.
Touch an ally and dispell any ongoing curse or magical effects on it.

[Feather Fall] : Reaction or 0 Actions
Spend 1 Charge.
Consume a {Large Feather} which can be bought for 10 gold.
Up to 2 creatures you choose within 6 meters will start falling gently, down to 3 meters per round.
Once the fall finishes, the spell effect ends.

[Fog Cloud] : 1 Action
Spend 1 Charge.
A 5x5x5 meter cube of fog forms with the center within 6 meters.
The fog spreads around corners.
You can choose the color of the fog.
All creatures inside the fog benefit from Cover, as well as creatures outside the fog attacked by a creature inside the fog.

[Radiant Smite] : 0 Actions
Spend 2 Charges.
After you land a melee attack, deal 1d8 True damage to the target and all enemies around the target.

[Blinding Smite] : 0 Actions
Spend 2 Charges.
After you land an attack, all creatures 1 meter in front of you and directly on your sides make a Dexterity save.
If they fail, they are Blinded for 1 turn.

[Cleansing Smite] : 0 Actions
Spend 2 Charges.
After you land a melee attack, deal 1d8 True damage to the target and heal yourself for that 1d8.

[Conceal Magic] : 1 Action (Level 4)
Spend 1 Charge.
Your magic becomes undetectable for 1 hour.

[Conjure Object] : 1 Action (Level 4)
Spend 1 Charge.
You can conjure an object that is up to 1 cube meter in size.
The object is solid and glows faintly.
You can't conjure mechanical objects.
The object disappears after 1 hour.

[Curse of Pain] : 1 Action
Spend 1 Charge.
A creature within 6 meters makes a Charisma save.
If it fails, it takes 1d4 Psychic damage whenever it makes an attack.
Lasts for 1 minute.

[Curse of Weakness] : 1 Action
Spend 1 Charge.
A creature within 6 meters makes a Charisma save.
If it fails, whenever it makes an attack roll or save, it rolls 1d4 and subtracts it from the result.
Lasts for 1 minute.

[Deathfire Bolt] : 1 Action
Spend 1 Charge.
Make a spell attack against a creature up to 6 meters away.
This attack deals 1d12 + 1d10 + 1d8 damage, half Fire, half Necrotic.

[Distort Vision] : 1 Action
Spend 1 Charge.
This incantation distorts the visual space in a 2x2 area.
Make a Deception check for this, and you can add your Intelligence to the roll.
While keeping the distorsion, you must maintain concentration.
You lose concentration if you take damage or become crowd controlled.

[Fairy Fire] : 1 Action
Spend 1 Charge.
Declare a 4x4x4 meter zone within 6 meters.
All creatures in that zone make a Dexterity save with Disadvantage.
If they fail, they become outlined in a colored glow (you choose the color).
The creatures glow even through walls, but the denser the walls, the less they appear to glow.
A 1-meter thick concrete wall completely blocks the glow.
Lasts up to 10 minutes.

[Fire Enchantment] : 1 Action
Spend 1 Charge.
Use a {Fire Rune} to imbue a weapon with Fire.
That weapon deals +2 Fire damage.
Lasts for 5 landed attacks, or for 24 hours.

[Flash] : 0 Actions
Spend 1 Charge.
Instantly move up to 6 feet away.
You must be able to get there as you normally would on foot.

[Frost Rune] : 1 Action
Spend 1 Charge.
Place a rune on a smooth solid surface. The rune covers a 1x1 area.
Heavy physical contact with the rune detonates it, dealing 3d6 Cold damage to the creature that activates it.
The creature also rolls a Dexterity save.
If it fails, it is Snared (frozen).
Lasts up to 8 hours.

[Explosion Rune] : 1 Action
Spend 1 Charge.
Place a rune on a smooth solid surface. The rune covers a 1x1 area.
Heavy physical contact with the rune detonates it, dealing 1d10 + 1 Fire or Force damage (you choose) to all creatures in a 3x3x3 meter cube around the rune.
Lasts up to 8 hours.

[Fake Sound] : 0.5 Action

4 meters range.

[Ice Spike] : 1 Action
Spend 2 Charges.

[Influence Beast] : 1 Action
Spend 1 Charge.
A non-legendary beast that can see you makes a Charisma save.

[Flame Strike] : 1 Action
Spend 1 Charge.
Conjure a 4x4 zone of flames on the ground, within 8 meters.
At the end of each of your turns, roll 1d8.
All creatures inside the zone take that amount as Fire damage.

[Invisibility] : 1 Action (Level 4)
Spend 1 Charge.
You become invisible for up to (Intelligence) minutes (at least 1).
Attacking, taking damage or casting spells while invisible breaks the invisibility.

[Levitate] : 1 Action (Level 4)
Spend 1 Charge.
First, roll 2d100. That's how many kilograms you can levitate when you cast this.
You can levitate one object you can see that remains within 6 meters.
You can slowly move the object vertically, 1 meter for each 2 of your movement points spent to levitate.
If you use this on an enemy, it must fail a Fortitude save to be levitated, and can repeat the save at the end of each of its turns.
A levitated creature counts as Snared.
Losing concentration (taking damage or using any action) instantly starts slowly dropping the target and breaks the Levitate.

[Mage Armor] : 1 Action
Spend 1 Charge.
For 8 Hours, your Armor can't be lower than 11.

[Barkskin] : Reaction
Spend 1 Charge and choose an ally within 6 meters.
That ally becomes rooted (snared) in the ground for up to 1 minute.
While rooted, that ally's Armor can't be lower than 12.
On your turn, you can cancel this effect at any point.

[Mage Hand] : 0 Actions
Spend 1 Charge.
Conjure a spectral hand in a place you can see within 6 meters.
You can move the hand as you move your normal hands and instead of spending movement points to move yourself, you can move your hand the same distance.
The hand can't attack and disappears if it goes outside a 12 meters range.
The hand lasts for up to 8 hours.

[Mending] : 1 Action
Spend 1 Charge.
Touch a slightly broken piece of material that is still in one piece.
You can repair the damage done to it.
Examples: repair a hole through a chain shirt or textile material, the crack in a piece of glass, etc.

[Unlock] : 1 Action
Spend 1 Charge.
You can attempt to unlock a lock magically from up to 9 meters away.
Using this counts as if you Know How to Pick Locks.
You roll a Sleight of Hand for this, and you get a +2 on it.

[Magic Prison] : 5 minutes (Level 8)
You create a 4x4x4 meter cube.
Choose a creature inside the cube.
It makes an Intelligence save with triple disadvantage.
If it fails, it can not exit the cube for 24 hours.
It can make this save to try to escape every 24 hours.
Lasts up to 7 days.
You can cast this once per day per charge.


[Portal] : 4 Hours (Level 8)
Spend 3 Charges.
You need a partner who can cast Portal to cast this.
You both cast it at the same time, in different locations.
At the end of the cast time, a portal opens at the locations of the two casters.
Stepping through one of the portals (obviously) teleports the creature to the other portal.
Lasts up to 30 days.
You can only cast this every 30 days.
